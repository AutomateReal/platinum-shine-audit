3 Things to Fix Before Running Any Ads or SEO
⚠ Zero Photos on a Pressure Washing Profile — That's the Entire Sales Pitch Missing
Pressure washing is a visual service. Before/after photos are the #1 conversion driver on a GBP listing — customers decide to call based on seeing the work. The profile currently has zero photos. Every job Dashon completes is a photo opportunity. Ten before/after shots would immediately put Kingdom ahead of most competitors in the suburb markets and give Google visual signals to surface the listing in image-forward searches.
⚠ No Business Hours and No Attributes — The Profile Looks Inactive
Google's algorithm treats a profile with no hours set as incomplete — and incomplete profiles rank below complete ones even with similar review counts. Kingdom also has zero attributes set. Attributes like "On-site services," "Free estimates," "Insured," and "Veteran-owned" (if applicable) are trust signals customers filter by and ranking signals Google uses. These take 5 minutes to set and have immediate impact.
⚠ No Physical Address Pin — Limits Suburb Rankings to Guesswork
Currently the profile is set as a service area business with no address pin. Google uses the registered address as the geographic anchor for local pack rankings. Without a pin, Google has to guess where to rank Kingdom — and it defaults to showing it far from where it actually operates. A verified address in Cypress or Katy gives Google a fixed anchor, and proximity is one of the three primary local pack ranking factors. This is the single highest-leverage structural fix available.
